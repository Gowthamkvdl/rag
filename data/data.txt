Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company. It is part of the Tata Group, one of India's largest business conglomerates.

Founded: 1968  
Founder: Tata Sons  
Headquarters: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India  

Services:
- IT Services
- Consulting
- Business Solutions
- Cloud Computing
- Artificial Intelligence
- Cybersecurity
- Enterprise Applications

Global Presence:
TCS operates in over 50 countries and serves clients across multiple industries including banking, healthcare, retail, and telecommunications.

Employees:
TCS is one of the largest IT employers in the world with over 600,000 employees.

Revenue:
TCS consistently ranks among the top IT services companies globally in terms of revenue.

Key Clients:
- Banking and Financial Institutions
- Government Organizations
- Retail Companies
- Healthcare Providers

Subsidiaries:
- TCS iON
- TCS Digital
- TCS BaNCS

Stock Listings:
- BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange)
- NSE (National Stock Exchange)

CEO:
K. Krithivasan

Achievements:
- One of the most valuable IT services brands globally
- Consistently ranked among top outsourcing companies
- Strong focus on innovation and digital transformation
